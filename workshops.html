Girls Rocketry Workshops

Historically, STEM has been dominated by boys, especially the field of aviation. This is changing quickly, and we want you to be part of this exciting movement! There aren’t enough STEM programs designed by girls for girls, which inspired us to create one. This program is designed to be fast-paced, engaging, and fun while still satisfying our curiosities and appetites to learn cool stuff. Our workshops are designed and taught by the co-presidents of Bethesda-Chevy Chase High School’s Rocketry Club who have a passion for making learning fun.

During each session, the girls will get to build and launch their very own model rockets as they learn about aviation and forces of flight.

Each four-week session will accept only 10 students. All materials (rocket kits, engines, and wadding) will be provided.
